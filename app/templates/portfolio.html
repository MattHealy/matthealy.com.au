foodloggr

foodloggr is a simple food logging web app. Traditional food logging apps require you to enter the amount of calories you consume which takes a lot of time, and quite frankly gets a bit boring after a while. foodloggr helps you to stay on track with your eating habits by allowing you to share your food log with your friends.

foodloggr is developed using the Flask framework for Python, and hosted on Amazon EC2 and Amazon S3 .

qrByte

qrByte is a free web-based tool for creating intelligent QR codes. The QR codes have logic built in to redirect user to the App Store or Google Play Store depending on what device they're using. Alternatively, you can create a QR code to link your visitors to all your social media accounts.

qrByte is developed using the Flask framework for Python, and hosted on Amazon EC2.
