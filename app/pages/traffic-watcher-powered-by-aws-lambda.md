On my daily commute I drive up and down the Kwinana Freeway, like thousands of other West Australians. Every now and then (sometimes it can seem like a daily occurrence!) the drive takes a little longer than expected due to some delay like a crash or debris on the road. It always annoyed me when I would hit a stop in traffic and not know what the problem was - should I get in the other lane, should I get off the freeway and go around the problem?

I'm a regular <a href="https://twitter.com/matt_healy">Twitter user</a> so I checked out the <a href="https://twitter.com/Perth_Traffic">Perth Traffic</a> feed. This feed is updated regularly throughout the day whenever an incident occurs or Main Roads WA needs to advise road users on an upcoming closure.
